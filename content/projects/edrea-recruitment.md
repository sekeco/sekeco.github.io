We developed Edrea Recruitment & Deployment Platform, a unified operations system for recruitment and staffing teams to manage candidate processing, documentation, finance, and deployment with stronger speed and control.

## Key Features

- **End-to-End Candidate Workflow**
    - Connected the full recruitment pipeline from applicant intake and screening to approval, visa handling, and final deployment
    - Reduced reliance on spreadsheets and fragmented handoffs across operational teams
    - Built structured workflow stages to improve consistency and execution discipline

- **Centralized Data and Document Management**
    - Organized candidate profiles, personal records, education history, work experience, and compliance files in one system
    - Simplified document tracking for operational readiness and auditability
    - Improved collaboration by making critical candidate data easier to access and maintain

- **Finance, Access Control, and Automation**
    - Linked billing and payment tracking to candidate milestones for better operational and financial alignment
    - Supported multi-company workflows with role-based permissions for admins, finance teams, and PICs
    - Added dashboards and scheduled automation to improve visibility, reduce repetitive work, and help teams stay proactive

## Impact and Success

Edrea Recruitment & Deployment Platform transformed a complex workforce placement process into a more measurable and scalable operation, helping teams move faster, maintain tighter process control, and gain clearer visibility into recruitment and deployment performance.

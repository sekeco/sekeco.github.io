We developed a custom WordPress theme for the Faculty of Islamic Studies at Universitas Islam Internasional Indonesia (FIS UIII) to strengthen institutional credibility through a modern, consistent, and easy-to-manage digital presence.

## Key Features

- **Institutional Branding System**
    - Built a cohesive visual identity system covering color palette, typography, spacing, and layout structure.
    - Maintained consistent presentation across faculty pages and content types.
    - Reinforced a professional and trustworthy institutional image.

- **Content-First Publishing Experience**
    - Designed templates for posts and single pages to support continuous publishing.
    - Optimized structure for news, announcements, articles, and academic updates.
    - Enabled editorial teams to keep content fresh without complex workflows.

- **Responsive Navigation and Layout**
    - Implemented adaptive navigation and content containers for desktop and mobile devices.
    - Improved accessibility of academic information for students, faculty, and public audiences.
    - Ensured stable browsing experience across screen sizes.

- **Block Editor and Developer Workflow**
    - Supported modern WordPress block editor for faster non-technical content updates.
    - Built front-end pipeline with Tailwind CSS and Laravel Mix for maintainable styling and asset builds.
    - Established scalable base for future theme enhancements.

## Impact and Success

This project delivered a strong production-ready foundation for faculty digital communications. By combining clear branding, editor-friendly publishing, and scalable front-end architecture, the theme improved content operations and positioned FIS UIII for long-term website growth and better audience engagement.

We developed Mustaka Lite to centralize fragmented school administration into unified admissions and operations platform. System covers registration, verification, interview scheduling, billing, payment tracking, and communication updates for school teams and prospective families.

## Key Features

- **Dual-Panel Experience**
    - Built dedicated admin panel for internal operations and admissions panel for applicants.
    - Separated complex back-office tasks from applicant journey.
    - Improved operational control while keeping user flow simpler for families.

- **Structured Multi-Wave Admissions Management**
    - Implemented wave-based admissions with configurable windows, requirements, and payment components.
    - Linked applicants automatically to selected wave and initialized related obligations.
    - Turned seasonal admissions into repeatable yearly workflow engine.

- **End-to-End Applicant Journey Tracking**
    - Added status milestones from submitted to verified to accepted to fully paid.
    - Included interview scheduling and progress indicators for staff and applicants.
    - Reduced repetitive status inquiries through clearer visibility.

- **Billing, Payment, and Financial Visibility**
    - Supported bill creation with line items, payment recording, and payment history.
    - Added officer and payment method tracking with dashboard monitoring.
    - Improved control over admissions cash flow and unpaid balances.

- **Communication and Information Delivery**
    - Implemented announcement publishing with active period windows.
    - Added practical communication helpers including WhatsApp link-based actions.
    - Helped schools provide more proactive and consistent update delivery.

## Impact and Success

Mustaka Lite transformed admissions operations from manual, fragmented process into structured digital pipeline. Platform improved administrative speed, increased financial visibility, and delivered more professional and transparent registration experience for families.

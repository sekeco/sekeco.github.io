We developed PT Qomar Sejahtera Land website as digital sales office that combines brand credibility, property discovery, and direct inquiry flow. Platform helps prospects evaluate projects faster through clear listing details and low-friction WhatsApp-based contact actions.

## Key Features

- **Brand-Led Landing Experience**
    - Designed high-impact homepage with trust-oriented messaging and visual storytelling.
    - Positioned company identity, values, and credibility in first-screen narrative.
    - Improved first impression for property buyers and investors.

- **Multi-Project Showcase with Smart Filtering**
    - Presented multiple projects across cluster, villa, griya, and pondok categories.
    - Added browsing and filtering by property type for faster discovery.
    - Helped prospects focus on relevant options with lower search friction.

- **Detailed Property Pages**
    - Provided location context, pricing, dimensions, room details, specifications, and galleries.
    - Included floor-plan support where available for better evaluation confidence.
    - Moved visitors from casual browsing to informed purchase consideration.

- **WhatsApp-First Conversion Flow**
    - Embedded inquiry actions across navigation, listing cards, floating CTA, and forms.
    - Converted contact form submissions into preformatted WhatsApp messages.
    - Accelerated lead handoff from website traffic to sales team response.

- **Trust Reinforcement and Company Presence**
    - Added company profile, vision-mission, social context, contact details, and map embed.
    - Strengthened perceived reliability in high-value property decisions.
    - Built more complete trust layer for first-time prospects.

## Impact and Success

PT Qomar platform transformed static company profile into conversion-oriented property marketing engine. Website improved trust communication, streamlined project discovery, and increased lead readiness by connecting information depth with direct WhatsApp contact flow.

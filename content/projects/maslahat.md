We developed Maslahat.id, a comprehensive digital platform focused on Islamic family values and providing guidance for Muslim families.

## Key Features

- **Brand Identity**
    - Designed a warm, family-friendly logo with Islamic aesthetics
    - Created brand guidelines emphasizing trust and community
    - Developed visual language suitable for diverse content types

- **Content Platform**
    - Built a robust content management system for articles and resources
    - Implemented multi-category organization (marriage, parenting, spirituality)
    - Created user-friendly navigation for easy content discovery
    - Integrated social sharing features
    - Developed mobile-responsive design for accessibility

- **Community Features**
    - Built discussion forums for community interaction
    - Implemented user accounts and profiles
    - Created bookmarking and favorites system
    - Added newsletter subscription functionality

## Impact and Success

Maslahat.id has become a trusted resource for Muslim families, with thousands of monthly visitors seeking guidance and support.

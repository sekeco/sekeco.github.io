We developed StokGudang to centralize warehouse operations and eliminate spreadsheet-driven stock control. Platform tracks inventory master data, inbound supply, outbound usage, and supplier context in structured admin workflow with automatic stock adjustment logic.

## Key Features

- **Centralized Inventory Master Data**
    - Stored item-level data including product, brand, unit, supplier, and current quantity.
    - Created single source of truth for inventory records.
    - Reduced duplicate and inconsistent stock entries.

- **Inbound Supply Tracking (Stock In)**
    - Implemented stock-in transactions with quantity, supplier, price, subtotal, and timestamp.
    - Added automatic subtotal calculations for faster operator input.
    - Improved speed and consistency during daily restock workflows.

- **Outbound Usage Tracking (Stock Out)**
    - Added dedicated flow for stock usage and cost events.
    - Captured quantity out, notes, and transaction timing.
    - Improved visibility into stock consumption patterns.

- **Automatic Quantity Adjustment Engine**
    - Increased stock automatically on supply records.
    - Decreased stock automatically on usage records.
    - Reduced manual calculation errors and improved quantity accuracy.

- **Operational Admin Experience**
    - Built searchable lists, filters, grouping, and action-based forms for routine tasks.
    - Added in-app notification support and quick supplier contact flow.
    - Improved procurement and replenishment response time.

## Impact and Success

StokGudang transformed warehouse control from manual reconciliation into reliable digital operation flow. Platform improved stock data accuracy, reduced admin friction, and gave teams faster operational decision support for day-to-day inventory management.

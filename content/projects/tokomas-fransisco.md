We developed Tokomas Fransisco, a digital operating system for gold shop operations that unifies customer purchases, customer sell-backs, pricing management, and transaction tracking in one structured workflow.

## Key Features

- **Transaction Management**
    - Managed both gold purchase and gold sell-back flows as separate but connected operational processes.
    - Structured transaction statuses, proof of payment handling, and customer records in a single system.
    - Reduced dependence on manual coordination through chats and spreadsheets.

- **Pricing and Order Calculation**
    - Stored buy and sell prices for each gold product with price history tracking.
    - Calculated order totals based on product type, quantity, and gram weight.
    - Helped minimize manual pricing errors in high-value transactions.

- **Admin and Customer Portals**
    - Built an admin panel for managing products, transactions, settings, and reporting.
    - Provided signed-in customers with dashboards, order placement, and order detail views.
    - Created a clearer operational flow for both internal staff and end customers.

- **Operational Controls and Reporting**
    - Added market open and close settings, gram-based transaction limits, and exportable records.
    - Supported status tracking and basic dashboard statistics for daily monitoring.
    - Improved visibility into transaction progress and business operations.

## Impact and Success

Tokomas Fransisco helped streamline gold trading operations by improving pricing accuracy, reducing human error, and creating a clearer audit trail for every transaction. The platform supports a working MVP with practical back-office and customer-facing workflows, giving the business a stronger operational foundation as it grows.

We partnered with PT. Tania Selatan to create a strong corporate identity and digital presence that reflects their commitment to sustainable palm oil production.

## Key Features

- **Corporate Identity**
    - Designed a professional logo representing growth and sustainability
    - Established comprehensive brand guidelines for consistent communication
    - Created corporate presentation templates and documentation

- **Digital Platform**
    - Developed a corporate website highlighting their sustainability initiatives
    - Implemented an investor relations portal
    - Created interactive plantation maps and data visualization tools
    - Built a news and media center for press releases

## Impact and Success

The project strengthened PT. Tania Selatan's corporate image and improved stakeholder communication, supporting their sustainability goals.

We built **Ihram**, a purpose-driven mobile platform for Muslim pilgrims that combines Islamic spiritual content, social community features, and smart location tools into a single, seamless experience. The platform consists of a cross-platform mobile app paired with a robust Laravel API and a full-featured admin dashboard — designed to serve individual pilgrims, community organizers, and platform administrators alike.

## Key Features

- **Islamic Content Library**
    - Curated library of guides, prayers, locations, and FAQs tailored to the pilgrimage journey.
    - Content is structured for easy discovery — filterable by tags, categories, and geolocation.
    - Supports rich media including audio files, images, and step-by-step guidance materials.
    - Empowers pilgrims to access the right knowledge at every stage of their journey, even offline.

- **Social Community & Friendship Network**
    - Pilgrims can connect, follow, and build friendships with fellow travelers from around the world.
    - Mutual friend discovery, friend-of-friend suggestions, and user blocking give users full control over their social experience.
    - Friend groups allow pilgrims to organize and stay connected with their travel companions.
    - A familiar social experience built specifically for the spiritual context of Hajj and Umrah.

- **Real-Time Geolocation & Nearby Discovery**
    - Users can share their live location with trusted friends and discover nearby pilgrims using GPS-based proximity search.
    - Location sharing is opt-in and user-controlled, prioritizing safety and privacy.
    - Sacred site locations are mapped and made explorable directly within the app.
    - Reduces anxiety of crowded pilgrimage environments by helping groups stay coordinated.

- **Subscription Plans & QRIS Billing**
    - Flexible subscription tiers unlock premium content and advanced features.
    - Full billing lifecycle management: plan selection, trial periods, grace periods, and invoice generation.
    - Integrated with **QRIS** — Indonesia's national QR payment standard — for seamless local payments.
    - Transparent usage tracking ensures users always know what they have access to.

- **Comprehensive Admin Panel**
    - A powerful Filament-based dashboard gives administrators full visibility and control over the platform.
    - Manage users, content, support tickets, subscription plans, and payment invoices from a single interface.
    - Built-in analytics and filtering tools streamline day-to-day operations.
    - Enables the business team to operate independently without relying on developers for routine tasks.

## Value Proposition

The pilgrimage is one of the most significant spiritual journeys a Muslim will undertake — yet pilgrims often struggle with fragmented information, lost group members, and limited access to reliable guidance in the moment they need it most.

Ihram addresses all of this in one place. By unifying Islamic educational content, community connectivity, real-time location tools, and a sustainable subscription model, the platform delivers genuine value to every type of user: the first-time pilgrim seeking guidance, the group leader keeping everyone safe, and the returning pilgrim staying connected to a global community year-round.

For the business, the subscription-based model creates a recurring revenue stream with a built-in growth path — as the community grows, so does the platform's value.

## Current Status

Ihram is a **production-ready platform**. The mobile application is fully developed and deployed, supported by a mature API that handles authentication, social interactions, content delivery, geolocation, and billing. The admin panel is operational, giving the team immediate oversight of the entire platform.

The codebase reflects a high standard of engineering: built on **Laravel 12** with **Laravel Octane** for high-concurrency performance, **Laravel Sanctum** for secure token-based authentication, automated API documentation via **Scramble**, and a clean modular architecture ready to scale. With 50+ API endpoints, 10+ admin resources, and a fully tested codebase, this is well past the MVP phase and positioned for growth.

## Next Development Opportunity

The most compelling next step is **expanding the platform's reach beyond Indonesia** by adding multi-language support for Arabic and English content — unlocking a global Muslim audience of over 1.8 billion people. Paired with internationalized payment gateways (Stripe, Tap Payments), Ihram could grow from a regional product into a globally recognized spiritual companion for every pilgrim, everywhere.

Additional high-impact opportunities include push notifications for journey milestones, AI-powered dua and guide recommendations, and group trip planning tools — all of which would deepen engagement and strengthen the platform's long-term retention.

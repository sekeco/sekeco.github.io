We developed KOPMA UGM platform to digitize cooperative operations by centralizing membership workflows, financial records, savings obligations, transactions, events, and documents in one system. Platform replaces chat-driven and spreadsheet-heavy processes with structured day-to-day management tools.

## Key Features

- **End-to-End Membership Lifecycle**
    - Built unified flow for candidate registration, profile completion, review, approval, and active member management.
    - Reduced onboarding friction as member volume grows.
    - Created consistent member data flow from recruitment to active operations.

- **Integrated Cooperative Finance Tracking**
    - Implemented wallet balance management with debit and credit transaction logging.
    - Added payment method tagging and savings transaction categorization.
    - Improved reconciliation and day-to-day financial visibility for cooperative teams.

- **Automated Saving Cycle Operations**
    - Supported periodic savings programs with default contribution rules.
    - Auto-generated member saving obligations for active members.
    - Reduced repetitive manual finance administration.

- **Member and Admin Dual Portals**
    - Separated admin operational tools from member self-service experience.
    - Gave management access to controls and analytics while members access profiles and financial history.
    - Improved transparency between organization and members.

- **Operational Content and Compliance Support**
    - Added document management for member-owned and organization-wide files.
    - Included event publishing and dashboard indicators for operational monitoring.
    - Strengthened structure for compliance and internal coordination.

## Impact and Success

KOPMA UGM platform transformed manual cooperative administration into clearer and more scalable digital workflow. System improved financial transparency, reduced reconciliation effort, and gave both management and members more reliable access to operational and personal cooperative data.

We developed CekStatus to reduce repetitive status inquiries in service businesses by providing transparent customer tracking portal and structured internal operations dashboard. Platform helps teams manage orders, status workflows, and organization-level collaboration while customers get real-time progress visibility.

## Key Features

- **Customer Self-Service Tracking Portal**
    - Enabled public tracking page using order number lookup.
    - Displayed status timeline, notes, attachments, and estimated completion info.
    - Reduced repetitive inbound status calls and messages.

- **Merchant Operations Dashboard**
    - Built order management workspace with create, search, filter, and activity monitoring tools.
    - Centralized daily service operations in one repeatable workflow.
    - Improved handling speed from order intake to completion.

- **Customizable Status Workflow and Timeline Events**
    - Supported organization-defined status templates and update milestones.
    - Standardized service communication across team members.
    - Reduced missed or inconsistent customer progress updates.

- **Multi-Organization Team and Access Management**
    - Implemented organization workspace model with member invites and role-based access.
    - Protected account boundaries while enabling team collaboration.
    - Improved governance for growing service teams.

- **Business Insights and Product Readiness**
    - Added KPI visibility for total orders, completions, pending work, and active customers.
    - Integrated authentication, security controls, and multi-language readiness.
    - Established strong MVP-plus foundation for SaaS growth.

## Impact and Success

CekStatus transformed status communication from reactive support burden into transparent customer experience layer. Platform improved trust, lowered repetitive communication workload, and gave service businesses scalable operational foundation for growth-oriented workflow management.

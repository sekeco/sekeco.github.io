We developed a complete brand identity for Little Giant, a dynamic startup looking to make a big impact in their industry.

## Key Features

- **Logo Design**
  - Created multiple logo concepts reflecting growth and ambition
  - Developed final logo with versatile applications
  - Designed logo variations for different use cases (horizontal, vertical, icon)

- **Brand Guidelines**
  - Established comprehensive brand guidelines document
  - Defined color palette with primary and secondary colors
  - Selected typography system for print and digital media
  - Created pattern library and graphic elements
  - Developed usage rules and dos/don'ts

- **Brand Applications**
  - Designed business cards, letterheads, and envelopes
  - Created social media templates and graphics
  - Developed presentation templates
  - Designed merchandise mockups

## Impact and Success

The strong brand identity helped Little Giant stand out in a competitive market and establish a memorable presence.

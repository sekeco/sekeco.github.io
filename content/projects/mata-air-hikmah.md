We created a comprehensive brand identity and digital platform for Yayasan Mata Air Hikmah, an Islamic educational foundation dedicated to community development.

## Key Features

- **Brand Identity**
    - Designed a meaningful logo incorporating Islamic calligraphy and modern aesthetics
    - Developed brand guidelines reflecting the foundation's values and mission
    - Created educational materials and promotional content

- **Website Development**
    - Built an informative website showcasing programs and activities
    - Implemented online donation system with secure payment gateway
    - Created student portal for course registration and materials
    - Integrated blog for educational content and community updates

## Impact and Success

The new digital presence increased community engagement and simplified program registration, enabling the foundation to reach more students.

We developed Saling Tumbuh to close trust gap in donation ecosystem by giving donors and organizations a transparent, end-to-end fundraising workflow. Platform combines campaign management, real-time progress visibility, payment automation, and verified fund withdrawal lifecycle in one system.

## Key Features

- **Campaign Management Hub**
    - Built multi-campaign management with goals, timelines, categories, tags, and status flow from draft to active to completed.
    - Enabled rich storytelling with campaign media and structured descriptions.
    - Improved planning visibility for organizations running parallel fundraising programs.

- **Multi-Channel Payment Infrastructure**
    - Integrated Xendit for QRIS, bank transfer, e-wallet, credit card, and crypto payment flows.
    - Implemented automated payment processing, fee calculation, and reconciliation.
    - Reduced checkout friction and improved donation conversion.

- **Real-Time Transparency and Milestone Tracking**
    - Built live progress dashboard for donors and organizers to monitor campaign performance and fund usage.
    - Added milestone-based tracking to break large targets into measurable progress checkpoints.
    - Increased donor engagement through clear, ongoing visibility of impact progress.

- **Withdrawal Verification and Operational Control**
    - Implemented auditable withdrawal requests with reason, amount, and recipient details.
    - Tracked full request lifecycle from pending to approved to processing to completed.
    - Reduced administrative overhead and improved accountability in fund disbursement.

- **Production-Ready Technical Foundation**
    - Delivered full-stack architecture with Nuxt 4 frontend, Nitro backend, TypeScript, Drizzle ORM, and MySQL.
    - Added multi-organization isolation, webhook reconciliation, Better Auth integration, and Resend notifications.
    - Established secure and scalable base for AI-powered campaign intelligence in next iteration.

## Impact and Success

Saling Tumbuh transformed fundraising operations into a transparent and trust-driven workflow. Platform gives donors clear fund visibility, gives organizations scalable campaign operations, and creates accountable infrastructure where transactions, milestones, and withdrawals are consistently traceable.

Great product photography can dramatically impact sales. Here are professional techniques we use to create compelling product images.

## Lighting is Everything

Natural light works beautifully, but controlled studio lighting offers consistency. Key tips:

- Use soft, diffused light to minimize harsh shadows
- Three-point lighting creates dimension
- Backlight can add drama and separation

## Composition Techniques

### Rule of Thirds

Position products at intersection points for visual interest.

### Negative Space

Allow breathing room around products for cleaner, more professional looks.

### Multiple Angles

Show products from various perspectives to give customers complete understanding.

## Styling and Props

Choose props that complement without overwhelming. The product should always be the star.

## Post-Processing

Professional editing enhances without deceiving:

- Color correction for accuracy
- Remove distractions
- Maintain realistic appearance

## Consistency Matters

Maintain consistent style across all product images for cohesive brand presentation.
